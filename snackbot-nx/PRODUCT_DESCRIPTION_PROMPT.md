# Product Description Generation Prompt for ChatGPT

Copy and paste this prompt into ChatGPT, replacing `[PRODUCT_NAME]` with each product name one at a time.

---

## Prompt for ChatGPT:

```
I need you to write a comprehensive 4-5 page product description for [PRODUCT_NAME]. This description will be used in a chatbot system that answers customer questions about products.

Please write a detailed, factual description that covers ALL of the following sections:

### 1. Product Overview (1-2 paragraphs)
- Full product name and brand
- Brief introduction and what makes it special
- Target audience
- Key selling points

### 2. Product Details & Specifications
- Product type and category
- Available variants/flavors (if any)
- Package sizes and weights available
- Packaging type (e.g., packet, jar, box)
- Shelf life/expiry information
- Storage instructions

### 3. Ingredients & Composition
- Complete list of ingredients (in order of quantity)
- Key ingredients and their purpose
- Any special ingredients or unique components
- Information about preservatives, additives, or natural ingredients

### 4. Allergen Information
- List all allergens present (e.g., gluten, milk, soy, nuts, etc.)
- Manufacturing facility allergen warnings
- "May contain" statements if applicable
- Suitable for vegetarians/vegans (if applicable)

### 5. Nutritional Information
- Detailed nutritional facts per serving and per 100g
- Calories/energy content
- Macronutrients (protein, carbohydrates, fats, fiber)
- Micronutrients (vitamins, minerals)
- Sugar content and salt/sodium content
- Any health claims or nutritional benefits

### 6. Pricing & Cost Information
- Price range (mention different pack sizes if applicable)
- Price in Indian Rupees (₹)
- Value proposition (price per gram/unit)
- Any bulk pricing or promotional pricing information
- Where it's typically priced (budget/mid/premium segment)

### 7. Flavor Profile & Taste
- Detailed taste description
- Texture and mouthfeel
- Flavor notes and characteristics
- Comparison to similar products (if helpful)
- What makes the taste unique

### 8. Usage Instructions & Serving Suggestions
- How to consume/prepare the product
- Serving suggestions
- Best ways to enjoy it
- Pairing suggestions (if applicable)
- Preparation time (for products that need preparation)

### 9. Manufacturing & Origin
- Manufacturer name and brand
- Country of origin
- Manufacturing process (brief overview)
- Quality standards and certifications
- Any heritage or brand history

### 10. Availability & Purchase Information
- Where to buy (online/offline retailers)
- Availability in different regions
- Online purchase links or platforms (if applicable)
- Stock availability information

### 11. Additional Information
- Environmental/sustainability information (if applicable)
- Awards or recognition
- Popularity and market position
- Customer reviews summary (general sentiment)
- Any other relevant information

---

**IMPORTANT GUIDELINES:**
- Write in clear, professional, and customer-friendly language
- Be factual and accurate - use real information about the product
- Each section should be substantial (at least 2-3 paragraphs where applicable)
- Include specific numbers, measurements, and details wherever possible
- Total length should be 4-5 pages (approximately 2000-2500 words)
- Use proper formatting with clear section headings
- Make it conversational enough for a chatbot to reference, but detailed enough to answer specific questions
- Include pricing information in Indian Rupees (₹)
- Be comprehensive - this will be the only source of information for answering customer queries

Please generate the description now for: [PRODUCT_NAME]
```

## Tips:

- Ask ChatGPT to format the output clearly with headings
- You can ask for adjustments if any section is missing or too brief
- Request specific pricing information if ChatGPT doesn't include it
- Verify allergen information is complete
- Ensure nutritional information includes all standard values
